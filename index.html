Policies & FAQ

How does Start Sharp™ work?

Choose your kit, reserve a Launch Slot, complete the intake form, and we build your package during your scheduled project window. You review, we revise, and you receive your finished files.

What is a Launch Slot?

A Launch Slot is your reserved project window. Payment and a completed intake form are required before your slot is confirmed. Custom sites require a 50% deposit to reserve, with the balance due at delivery.

Do I own what you build?

Yes. Everything is built as launch-ready work and handed off to you after final approval. For custom coded sites, you own every file and can host it anywhere. Clients are responsible for their own domain, hosting, subscriptions, and future updates unless ongoing maintenance is purchased separately.

What if I need changes?

Each kit includes the revision rounds listed on its card. Major redesigns, new concepts, or a full change in business direction may require an additional fee, quoted before any extra work begins.

Are refunds available?

Because each package is custom-built, payments are non-refundable once work begins. If you need to cancel before your Launch Slot starts, you may request to reschedule.

Can you guarantee sales?

No. Start Sharp™ helps you look official, communicate clearly, and launch with stronger materials — but sales and business results are not guaranteed.
