TapSharp FAQ

Do my customers need an app?

No. NFC is built into modern iPhones and Android phones — your customer just holds their phone near the tag and your page opens. No app, no account, nothing to download.

Does it work on iPhone and Android?

Yes. Every tag is tested on both before it ships. Most phones from the last several years tap out of the box.

What can a tag link to?

Anything with a web link — your Google reviews, online menu, booking page, socials, a promotion, or your website. One tag can point anywhere, and you can change it whenever you like.

Can I change where a tag points later?

Yes. Sign in to your dashboard and repoint any tag in seconds. The physical tag on your counter never changes — only where it sends people.

Where do the tags go?

They're quarter-sized and stick to counters, tables, windows, menus, or a stand. Put them wherever a customer is already looking.

Do you place them for me?

Tags ship programmed, tested, and ready to stick, so most businesses set them up in minutes. If you'd rather we do it, on-site placement is $249 within 50 miles of Tulsa, OK; locations beyond that are quoted separately.
