This Privacy Policy explains how Verbum LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), which operates Start Sharp™, TapSharp™, and Phoebe, collects, uses, and shares information when you visit startsharpco.com or contact us. By using the site, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us. When you fill out a form (project intake, TapSharp inquiry, or request-info) or email us, we collect what you provide — such as your name, email address, phone number, business name, and details about your project.
  • Payment information. Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not collect or store your full card number; Stripe handles that directly under its own privacy policy.
  • Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we and our providers automatically receive certain technical data — such as IP address, browser and device type, pages viewed, and referring links — through cookies and similar technologies.

Cookies and tracking

We use the Meta (Facebook) Pixel on our pages to understand how visitors use the site and to measure and improve our advertising. The pixel may set cookies and share limited activity data with Meta. You can manage ad personalization in your Meta ad settings and control cookies through your browser settings.

Our pages also load fonts from Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address as part of serving those fonts.

How we use information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request;
  • To process payments and deliver and manage your projects, tags, or subscriptions;
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the website;
  • To measure and improve our marketing; and
  • To comply with legal obligations.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers that help us operate — for example, Stripe (payments), Meta (advertising/analytics), our email and hosting providers, and, where you use it, the TapSharp dashboard. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data retention

We keep personal information for as long as needed to provide our services, meet legal and accounting requirements, and resolve disputes. You can ask us to delete information we hold about you (see below).

Your choices

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information you have given us, or opt out of marketing messages, by emailing startsharp@verbumco.com. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA; contact us and we will do our best to honor them.

Children's privacy

The site is intended for business owners and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-party links

Our site may link to other websites (such as Stripe checkout or your own destinations). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the "Last updated" date above.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Email startsharp@verbumco.com.
